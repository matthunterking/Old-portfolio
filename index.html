NeighbourGood

Group Project - 1 week

For this project we were challenged to create a full stack web application using AngularJS. Working in a team with two other students we produced a job board where users can volunteer to help other people from their community with day to day tasks. Users can filter jobs by location and manage applicants for their posted jobs. I worked primarily on the map elements, styling and automated testing. Creating the functionality for the distance filtering was a challenge and involved reading a lot of documentation, however I was really pleased that I managed to include the feature as it really adds to the site.

Destination Love

Group Project - 1 week

Destination Love is a dating site which solves the dilemma of deciding where you and a potential partner can go on your first date. The app allows users to manage match requests, profiles and messages. Date suggestions are within a 1km radius of the geographical center between both users. I primarily worked on the user registration process, match request handling, messaging and the styling of the site. Initially I’d planned to use a third party package to manage sending and accepting matches however after running into difficulties decided it would be more fun and challenging to create my own system to manage theses requests.

About

At the start of 2018 I decided that I needed to change careers and having had a professional life of always being the only person in the office who knew how to work Microsoft Excel, decided that web development would be a good avenue to explore.

After a couple of online courses I was hooked and decided to take a three month immersive course at General Assembly.

Following my completion of the course I was asked to become part of the instructional team for the next two groups of students. This gave me the opportunity to solidify my coding knowledge as well as help and coach new students.

Web development provides me with fresh and exciting challenges everyday and I love pushing myself to solve problems and learn new skills.
