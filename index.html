About

After a couple of online courses I was hooked and decided to take a three month immersive course at General Assembly.

This gave me the opportunity to solidify my coding knowledge as well as coach and assist new students.

Web development provides me with fresh and exciting challenges everyday and I love pushing myself to solve problems and learn new skills.
